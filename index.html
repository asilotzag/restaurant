the otzag history

more than 25+ years of experiance

otzag restaurant is a culinary landmark rich in history and storytelling. It began with a girl's simple dream of making a positive impact in the food world. From humble roots, the restaurant evolved, blending tradition with innovation. Each dish tells a unique culinary story of love, care, and hard work. Join us on this captivating journey where flavors delight and traditions endure.
